AKADEMIYA

The Sumeru Akademiya is Sumeru's main governing body, as well as the most prestigious institute of learning in all of Teyvat, located in the
middle of the tree trunk of Sumeru City. It is particularly renowned for its corpus in arcane arts and historical records.

DARSHANS WE SUPPORT

Sumeru Akademiya is currently divided into Six Darshans, each of which has a specialty and a designated color for their uniform.The first Darshans
were established at the same time as the Akademiya itself by Greater Lord Rukkhadevata under the name of Six Great Schools, each representing a type of wisdom.

AMURTA

It is specialized in biology, and includes related disciplines such as ecology and medicine. The recognized color is green. The mascot on the school's symbol resembles a Huma bird.

RTAWAHIST

It is specialized in illuminationism, the study of the stars, and includes disciplines such as astronomy and astrology. Its school color is blue. The mascot on its symbol resembles an elephant.

SPANTAMAD

It is specialized in elementalism, the study of the elements, and includes related disciplines such as Ley Lines studies and alchemy. Its school color is red. The mascot on its symbol resembles a peacock with seven feathers and a tree beneath,

HARAVATAT

It is specialized in semiotics, the study of sign processes and meaning-making, and includes disciplines such as linguistics and ancient runes studies. Their recognized color is black. The mascot on their symbol resembles a bull.

VAHUMANA

It is specialized in aetiology, the study of causation or origination of phenomena, and includes disciplines such as history and social sciences. Its school color is yellow. The mascot on its symbol resembles a horse.

KSHAHREWAR

It is specialized in technology,and includes related disciplines such as architecture and puzzle-solving. Its school color is white. The mascot on its symbol resembles a lion.
